Loudoun County Regional Science and Engineering Fair

In this fair, my partner and I conducted an experiment to test the effects of low gravitational levels on plants that were dominated by either phototropism or gravitropism(geotropism). For this project, my friend and I won the AIAA award, in which we were granted a 3 day scholarship program at NASA Goddard Space Flight Center to tour facilities closed to the public. In addition, we were invited to join the top aerospace officials at the AIAA Capital Sector Banquet.

American Computer Science League

I was part of my school ACSL Team and was within the top 5 programmers in the group. As a result, I was selected to go to the All-Star ACSL Competition, in which the top 5 students from each school compete with other schools in an ACSL-Contest Manner.

Accomplishments/Interests

  • NASA Scholarship

    In this program, I was able to tour NASA Goddard Space Flight Center facilities that were closed to the public and opened only once every 5 to 10 years. In addition, I was able to tour the Wallops Island NASA Facilites on Wallops Island, and talk to the people who work there about their projects and inventions. I was able to see the manufacturing of the James Webb Telescope Mirrors, along with the rooms and machines used to test the quality of the products.

  • Virginia Math League

    In this competition, I won third place at my school out the 40 kids who took the test. My score was 32/36 and I was recognized along with my peers as T4th/25th in the Virginia Math League School Competition during the 2013-2014 school year.

  • National Latin Exam

    In this exam, I was awarded the cum laude award for scoring 26 out of 35 points on the exam. This exam is administered by Cambridge University as students compete against each other by scoring higher than one another on the exam. Only few students from each grade level win such prestigious awards per Latin Course.

  • Varsity Tennis

    I made the school Varsity Tennis Team as a freshman was a seeded player on the team. I was selected to play in the school conference tournament and won both my singles matches and one out of the two doubles matches in the conference semifinals.

  • Volunteering

    I have logged over 50 volunteering hours at various events, such as the local library, school events, and at religious centers such as my local temples. I help in a lot of ways, such as running events and helping with set up and clean up. At the religious centers, I participate with priests in various religious rituals such as the recitation of scriptures and prayers. It is a very rewarding experience as volunteering helps me give back to the community, as it has helped me in the past as well.
