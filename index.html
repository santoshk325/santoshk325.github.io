Loudoun County Regional Science and Engineering Fair

In this fair, my partner and I conducted an experiment to test the effects of low gravitational levels on plants that were dominated by either phototropism or gravitropism(geotropism). For this project, my friend and I won the AIAA award, in which we were granted a 3 day scholarship program at NASA Goddard Space Flight Center to tour facilities closed to the public. In addition, we were invited to join the top aerospace officials at the AIAA Capital Sector Banquet.

American Computer Science League

I was part of my school ACSL Team and was within the top 5 programmers in the group. As a result, I was selected to go to the All-Star ACSL Competition, in which the top 5 students from each school compete with other schools in an ACSL-Contest Manner.

American Mathematics Competition

In this competition, I was awarded the national Honor Roll Merit award for scoring 18/25 on the AMC.
